Authorized Entities

Please note that this page is updated to reflect changes associated with the Utah Supreme Court’s new Phase 2 guidance.

Below is a list of companies and organizations authorized to operate in the Utah legal regulatory sandbox (“the Sandbox”) along with their authorization orders from the Utah Supreme Court (click on the name to view).

The Sandbox was launched by the Utah Supreme Court in 2020 and is a seven-year regulatory pilot project and experiment to increase access to legal services in Utah.  The authorization orders contain information on the areas of law a company or organization can practice in as well as the type of provider.  Types of providers authorized in the Sandbox include community justice advocates, other nonlawyer providers, and technology-based services such as AI.  Companies and organizations may also engage in fee-sharing with nonlawyers, including having nonlawyer ownership.  These authorization orders are limited to legal services in Utah, and operations for the Sandbox are currently housed in the Utah State Bar.

If are seeking legal help in Utah and have questions about a company or organization listed below, please email innovationoffice@utahbar.org.  If you have received legal help from one of these companies or organizations, we are in the process of implementing a new feedback form and consumer survey.
